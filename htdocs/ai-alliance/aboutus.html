We are a group of five *exceptional* young undergraduate female students who are currently partaking in the ai4socialgood lab at McGill University.

Most of us have a computer science background and we are steadily learning AI technologies throughout the lab.

This project is done in the context of the ai4socialgood Hackathon 2018, where we are able to apply both our old and newfound knowledge.

Using data acquired on the Internet, we trained an AI that will recognize Braille characters and translate it into english.

This is to help those who have their sight and wish to better assist the blind people around them.
